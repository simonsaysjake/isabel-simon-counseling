Therapy From Wherever You Are

Life is busy, and getting to a therapist's office isn't always easy. With telehealth counseling, you can attend your sessions from the comfort of your home, your car on a lunch break, or anywhere you feel safe and private. There's no commute, no sitting in traffic, and no rearranging your entire day.

Because Isabel is licensed to practice throughout California, online therapy is available to you no matter where you live in the state. Whether you're in the South Bay, Los Angeles, San Francisco, San Diego, or a smaller town in between, virtual therapy brings support directly to you.

All you need is a private space, a stable internet connection, and a device with a camera. It really is that simple.

How Online Therapy Works

Virtual therapy sessions with Isabel use a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform designed specifically for healthcare. Your privacy and confidentiality are protected at every step, just as they would be in an in-person session.

Here's what to expect:

  • You'll receive a secure link before each session -- just click to join at your scheduled time.
  • Sessions are the same length and follow the same structure as in-person appointments.
  • The video connection is encrypted and meets all federal privacy standards for telehealth.
  • If you run into any technical issues, Isabel will walk you through troubleshooting or offer a phone backup.

Research consistently shows that online therapy is just as effective as in-person therapy for most concerns. The quality of the relationship between you and your therapist matters far more than the medium, and that connection comes through clearly on screen.

Is Virtual Therapy Right for You?

Online counseling can be a great fit for many people. You might benefit from telehealth therapy if you:

  • Have a busy schedule and need flexibility around work, school, or caregiving.
  • Live in a part of California that's far from a therapist's office.
  • Feel more comfortable opening up from a familiar space like your own home.
  • Experience anxiety about visiting new places or being in clinical settings.
  • Travel frequently but want to keep your therapy consistent.
  • Simply prefer the convenience of not having to drive to an appointment.

Studies have found that virtual therapy is equally effective as in-person sessions for treating anxiety, depression, and many other common concerns. What matters most is that you feel comfortable and supported, and telehealth counseling makes that possible for more people than ever.

What Isabel Offers Online

Isabel provides individual counseling through telehealth for a wide range of concerns. Whether you're working through something specific or just feeling stuck, virtual sessions offer the same thoughtful, personalized care you'd receive in person.

Areas of focus include:

  • Anxiety -- learning to manage worry, overwhelm, and nervous energy.
  • Depression -- finding your way back to motivation, joy, and connection.
  • Life transitions -- navigating change like a new job, a move, a breakup, or a shift in identity.
  • Young adult concerns -- figuring out who you are and where you're headed in your 20s and 30s.
  • Eating disorders and body image -- building a healthier relationship with food and your body.

Isabel draws from evidence-based approaches including cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based techniques, and narrative therapy. These methods are well-suited to the virtual setting and have been shown to be highly effective in online formats.
