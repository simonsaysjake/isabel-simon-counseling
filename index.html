About Isabel

A therapist who truly listens

Isabel Simon is a clinical mental health counselor based in sunny California, with roots that stretch back to London. She comes from a lineage of female mental health professionals, carrying forward a family tradition of compassionate care and deep understanding of the human experience.

With a unique background in both art history and clinical mental health counseling, Isabel brings a creative, perceptive lens to her practice. She has experience working in eating disorder treatment at the PHP and IOP level, deepening her understanding of the connection between mind, body, and recovery. Her approach is warm and grounded, infused with appropriate humor that creates a space where clients feel genuinely at ease.

I believe in a holistic wellness model that addresses every dimension of who you are—physical, social, mental, and emotional—because healing happens when the whole person is seen.

Therapeutic Approach

How we'll work together

Drawing from evidence-based modalities, each session is tailored to your unique needs and goals.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Identifying and reshaping thought patterns that influence your emotions and behaviors, building practical coping strategies.

Mindfulness

Cultivating present-moment awareness to reduce anxiety, enhance self-compassion, and build emotional resilience.

Strengths-Based Therapy

Focusing on your innate strengths and resources to foster empowerment, self-efficacy, and positive change.

Narrative Therapy

Exploring and reauthoring the stories you tell about yourself to create more empowering life narratives.

Trauma-Informed Care

Creating a safe, supportive space that honors your experiences and gently guides the path toward healing.
